Technical Questions

MyAnime works on most modern devices including Windows and Mac computers, iOS and Android smartphones and tablets, and smart TVs with web browsers.

Buffering issues are usually related to your internet connection. Try the following:

  • Check your internet speed
  • Close other applications using bandwidth
  • Try lowering the video quality in the player settings
  • Refresh the page or try a different browser

Please use the contact form on our About page to report any technical issues. Include details about your device, browser, and the problem you're experiencing.
